Where I Teach

Currently I am teaching in Junior High School of TAYABAS WESTERN ACADEMY. Today, I commemorate my 3 years of teaching here. In those three years I can say that there's a lot of improvement and changes, there's a lot of sacrifices and patience because my work is my vocation. Giving the right and quality education to my learners is my aim, I want them to learn something everyday is my goal and those learnings they earned is they can use it in real life,which is my objective.


Being teacher is really hard, making lesson plan, dealing with diverse learners everyday, the pressure that the society expect in, making my self proud, achieving my dreams, facing personal and professional challenges and many more but something make me realize that all of it was a part only when I saw that my learners are learning and hearing "thankyou ma'am" melt my heart and gave energy to my tired body.


"Teacher Laika"
